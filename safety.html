Customer Safety - Our Priority

Background-Checked Drivers

All drivers on our platform go through a rigorous background screening process. This includes:

  • Police verification
  • Driving record checks
  • Proof of valid driver's license and insurance

COVID-19 Safety Measures

To ensure the safety of our customers and drivers, we follow strict health and safety guidelines:

  • Mandatory masks for drivers during rides
  • Vehicles are sanitized after each ride
  • Drivers are regularly tested for COVID-19 symptoms
  • Contactless payments are encouraged

24/7 Customer Support

We offer 24/7 customer support to assist you with any concerns or issues during your trip. You can reach us via:

  • Phone: 1800-123-456
  • Email: support@driveme.com
  • In-app chat support

In-App SOS Button

For your peace of mind, our app features a built-in SOS button that connects you directly to emergency services in case of any emergency during the ride.

Driver Training

All our drivers undergo specialized training to ensure a safe and courteous driving experience. This includes:

  • Safe driving practices
  • Customer service training
  • Dealing with emergencies

Insurance Coverage

We provide insurance coverage for both drivers and passengers to ensure everyone is protected in case of accidents or unforeseen circumstances.

Ratings and Reviews

After every ride, customers can rate drivers and provide feedback. Our system monitors driver ratings and ensures that only top-rated drivers remain on the platform.
